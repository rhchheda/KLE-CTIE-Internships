How it works
Nine structured stages. Both sides protected by automated SLA enforcement.
1

Startup posts role

Structured JD with discipline, qualification, skills, period, and stipend. Goes live immediately.

2

Student applies

No account needed. Institute email + resume + 20-word interest note. App ID issued instantly.

3

Startup reviews

Resume and application inline. Shortlist or reject with one click. Email sent both ways.

SLA: 7 days → auto-close at 14
4

Interview scheduled

Date, time, mode, Meet link. Calendar invite to both student email addresses.

SLA: 5 days → auto-close at 10
5

Outcome entered

Selected / Not Selected / Reschedule (max 2). Selection triggers OTP to institute email.

SLA: 48h nudge → auto-close at 96h
6

OTP confirmation

Student enters 6-digit OTP from institute email only. Binding acceptance. No paperwork.
